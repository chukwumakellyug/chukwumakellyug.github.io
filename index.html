What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and data from digital attacks. It encompasses preventive technologies, threat detection, and incident response to ensure your digital assets remain secure.

Current Threats

Understanding the landscape of modern cyber threats

🎣
Phishing
Social engineering attacks designed to steal credentials and sensitive information.
🔐
Ransomware
Malicious software that encrypts data and demands payment for decryption keys.
⚡
Zero-Day Exploits
Attacks exploiting unknown vulnerabilities before patches are available.

Security Tips

Essential practices for staying safe online

🔑
Strong Passwords
Use unique, complex passwords with 12+ characters including uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols.
📱
Two-Factor Auth
Enable 2FA on all accounts for an additional layer of security beyond passwords.
🔄
Regular Updates
Keep all software and systems patched with latest security updates immediately.
⚠️
Verify Links
Hover over links before clicking, verify sender addresses, and watch for phishing attempts.
